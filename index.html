About me

I work at the intersection of large-scale cloud infrastructure, AI systems, and regulated healthcare environments. My current focus is the architecture of agentic AI systems that can be governed, evaluated, observed, audited, and safely integrated into real enterprise workflows.

My professional background spans Oracle Health and Amazon Web Services, with public coverage describing work across healthcare AI, Amazon Bedrock, multimodal AI infrastructure, CloudFront, Rekognition, Textract, and reliable autonomous systems.

This site is the canonical public profile for my work. It collects verified source links, professional service, affiliations, and selected public coverage in one place so readers can connect the same person, field, and body of work.
